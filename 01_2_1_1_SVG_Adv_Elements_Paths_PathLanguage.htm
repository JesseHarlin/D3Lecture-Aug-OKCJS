Advanced Elements - Paths Explained:

[they speak their own mini - language.]


The "D" attribute.



Capital is absolute coordinates

  • M - Move to
  • L - Line to
  • H - Horizontal Line
  • V - Vertical Line
  • Z - Close the Path

M 50 50 h 200 L 150,200 v -100 z

  • Go to point 50,50
  • relatively, move horizontally forward 200
  • Draw a line to the absolute point 150,200
  • Draw a vertical line 'up' the y axis 100 pts
  • close the shape with z
